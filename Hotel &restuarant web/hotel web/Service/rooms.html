Ordinary Room:

These are the standard rooms offered by the hotel. They are usually the most basic and budget-friendly option. Ordinary rooms typically have essential amenities such as a bed, basic furniture, and a private bathroom with standard fixtures. Decor and furnishings are often simple and functional, with minimal frills or luxury touches. Views from ordinary rooms may vary and might not offer any specific scenic outlook.

Superior Room:

Superior rooms are a step up from ordinary rooms and offer enhanced comfort and amenities. They are usually larger in size compared to ordinary rooms. Superior rooms may include additional features such as a seating area, work desk, or upgraded bathroom amenities. Decor and furnishings in superior rooms tend to be more stylish and modern, with attention to detail and comfort. Superior rooms may offer better views or locations within the hotel, such as higher floors or scenic vistas.

Exquisite Room:

Exquisite rooms are the highest tier of accommodation offered by the hotel, often reserved for guests seeking luxury and indulgence. These rooms are typically the largest and most lavishly appointed in the hotel. Exquisite rooms may include special amenities such as a separate living area, Jacuzzi tub, private balcony or terrace, and premium bedding. Decor and furnishings in exquisite rooms are often luxurious, with high-quality materials, custom-designed furniture, and upscale finishes. Guests staying in exquisite rooms may enjoy exclusive perks such as complimentary breakfast, access to a private lounge or concierge service, and priority reservations for hotel facilities and services.
